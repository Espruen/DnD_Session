\chapter*{Xucphr}

\section{Spells}

\DndSpellHeader%
  {Check Engine Light}
  {5th-level Evocation}
  {1 action}
  {20 feet}
  {V, S, M (ink and parchment, which the spell consumes)}
  {Concentration, Up to 5 minutes}
  {\textbf{Attack/Save}: WIS 13}\\\
  
  
	You alter time around up to six creatures of your choice in a 40-foot Cube within range. Each target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be affected by this spell for the duration.
	
	An affected target’s Speed is halved, it takes a \textminus 2 penalty to AC and Dexterity saving throws, and it can’t take Reactions. On its turns, it can take either an action or a Bonus Action, not both, and it can make only one attack if it takes the Attack action. If it casts a spell with a Somatic component, there is a 25 percent chance the spell fails as a result of the target making the spell’s gestures too slowly.
